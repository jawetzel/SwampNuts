OUR PRODUCTS
Boiled
Swamp Nuts Cajun Hot Boiled Peanuts - Tender, bold, and deeply Cajun. Each peanut is soaked in a perfectly balanced Cajun brine with spices like red pepper, garlic, and bay. This blend gives a subtle heat that’s layered with Southern flavor, offering an authentic taste of Louisiana with every bite. Perfect for snack lovers looking to experience a true Cajun classic.
Order Now
Sweet
Sweet Swamp Nuts - We take sugar and Cajun Seasoning, bring it to a boil, and once it reaches the right consistency we mix in seasoned slow roasted peanuts and let it cool to end up with a sweet, savory, and spicy candy that you cannot find anywhere else.
Order Now
Roasted
Roasted Swamp Nuts - Crisp, flavorful, and unmistakably Cajun. Each peanut is brined in a rich, Cajun-inspired blend, then slow-roasted to a golden crunch. Finished with a balanced mix of savory spices like red pepper, garlic, and lemon, these roasted nuts are perfect for anyone craving a bold, satisfying snack that’s packed with Southern character.

Swamp Nuts is a small, family-owned business proudly rooted in Prairieville, Louisiana. Born out of a love for authentic Cajun flavors, we’re passionate about sharing the true taste of the South with our community and beyond. Every batch is made from scratch using fresh, high-quality ingredients, and crafted with a dedication to tradition that honors the spirit of Louisiana.

Our peanuts are seasoned with a unique blend of spices inspired by Cajun crawfish boils, delivering layers of flavor with every bite. Whether you’re a long-time local or discovering Cajun cuisine for the first time, Swamp Nuts invites you to experience the bold, savory, and irresistible flavors that define our home.

All of our products are homemade, vegetarian, and vegan-friendly. From our kitchens to your table, we’re committed to bringing you the highest quality, hand-prepared peanuts that capture the essence of a Southern boil in every bite. So grab a bag, savor the flavors, and let us bring a taste of Prairieville to you!
